BASKIN ROBBINS

I have tried to collect some of the rococo paintings and creat a mood board for my design .The use of pastel colors was very common during the Rococo Period. As there was the use of lights and darks.The use of dusty shades of blue, green, brown and beige in Rococo paintings creates a intimate atmosphere compared to the brighter, more vibrant color palettes often associated with the period. These muted tones evoke a sense of nostalgia.
